## What this is

Twelve installable skills that turn Claude into an operating partner for the small business owner. Sales pipeline hygiene, cold outreach discipline, GHL workflow templates, brand voice consistency, ghost-writer-me, meeting prep, weekly network review, weekly review, decision log, end-of-day review, plus the two new v1.1.0 additions: coaching call prep and local SEO.

Built for solopreneurs through 25-employee businesses running on GoHighLevel or HubSpot.

## The twelve skills

| # | Skill | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `weekly-network-review` | Friday review of every relationship touched this week. |
| 2 | `sales-pipeline-hygiene` | Kill zombies, advance stalled deals, reset stages. |
| 3 | `cold-outreach-discipline` | Write outreach in the operator's voice, one send at a time. |
| 4 | `brand-voice-consistency` | Enforce the voice standard across every draft. |
| 5 | `ghl-workflow-templates` | Deploy or update GHL automations from the operator's canonical library. |
| 6 | `meeting-prep` | 15-minute pre-meeting brief loaded with context. |
| 7 | `end-of-day-review` | Close the day. What shipped, what's blocked, tomorrow's top 3. |
| 8 | `weekly-review` | Friday full-week review. |
| 9 | `decision-log` | Permanent decisions captured with alternatives and expected outcome. |
| 10 | `ghost-writer-me` | Draft in the operator's own voice, tuned to context. |
| 11 | `coaching-call-prep` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Pre-call brief and structured agenda for coaching, consulting, or advisory calls. |
| 12 | `local-SEO` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Local search audit and fix list: Google Business Profile, NAP consistency, review velocity, landing page structure, keyword targets. |
