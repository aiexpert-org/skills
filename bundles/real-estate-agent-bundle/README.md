## What this is

Thirteen installable skills for real estate agents. Showing prep, MLS follow-up, deal doc review, referral tracking, transaction milestones, listing presentation, buyer consultation, open house plan, neighborhood brief, FSBO outreach, agent weekly review, plus the two new v1.1.0 additions: open-house lead capture and first-showing checklist.

Built for solo agents through 5-agent teams. Positioned entirely under the AI Expert brand.

## Anthropic Recommends

Anthropic has not shipped a real-estate-tier default set. AI Expert ships the first canonical set. The Real Estate Agent Bundle extends Anthropic's baseline (Essential Bundle) with:

- Every action that touches a live CRM contact or MLS follow-up sequence routes through `human-approval-gate` (Tier 2 — batch-approvable).
- The `open-house-lead-capture` sequence is designed with the human-approval gate integrated into the day-3 voicemail and day-7 triage step.
- The `first-showing-checklist` is an observation and diagnostic skill; no writes happen without operator confirmation.

## The thirteen skills

| # | Skill | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `showing-prep` | Prep the second-and-subsequent showing with property one-pagers and buyer preference notes. |
| 2 | `mls-follow-up` | Sequence the property-preference match sends between tours. |
| 3 | `deal-doc-review` | Review contracts, addenda, and closing docs for red flags. |
| 4 | `referral-tracking` | Track and nurture the agent's referral network. |
| 5 | `transaction-milestones` | Milestone-by-milestone checklist from offer to closing. |
| 6 | `listing-presentation` | Draft the listing presentation with the seller's specific priorities loaded. |
| 7 | `buyer-consultation` | Structure the initial buyer consultation. |
| 8 | `open-house-plan` | Pre-event plan for the open house itself. |
| 9 | `neighborhood-brief` | Neighborhood-level brief with recent sales, schools, and comparable inventory. |
| 10 | `fsbo-outreach` | Outreach sequence for FSBO listings. |
| 11 | `agent-weekly-review` | Agent-tier weekly review. |
| 12 | `open-house-lead-capture` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Digital-first sign-in, same-day follow-up, 7-day nurture, and 4-way triage. |
| 13 | `first-showing-checklist` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Pre-showing prep, in-showing observation prompts, post-showing debrief with 3 questions, and offer-readiness categorization. |
