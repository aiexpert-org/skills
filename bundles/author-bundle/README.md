Install the Essential Bundle first. This bundle assumes the universal foundation is already in place.

## What this is

Twelve installable skills for nonfiction authors before, during, and after book launch. Book outline shaper, chapter draft, podcast guest prep, keynote script, launch runway, testimonial request, evergreen pipeline, interrogate decision, plus the four v1.1.0 additions: manuscript audit, book endorsement outreach, book pitch for guest appearance, and newsletter list warming.

This bundle is the Author half of what was previously the Author-Speaker-Coach Bundle. The Podcast Host half moved to a separate bundle. Coaching-tier skills moved to the Small Business Owner Bundle.

## Anthropic Recommends

Anthropic has not shipped an author-tier default set. AI Expert ships the first canonical set. The Author Bundle extends Anthropic's baseline (Essential Bundle) with:

- Every draft or send routes through `human-approval-gate` — no post going out without the author's explicit yes.
- Manuscript audits are read-only diagnostics; they surface a fix list but do not modify the manuscript file.
- Endorsement and podcast pitches are drafted for review, never sent automatically.

## The twelve skills

| # | Skill | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `book-outline-shaper` | Structure the nonfiction book outline into three or five parts with a clear through-line. |
| 2 | `chapter-draft` | Draft a chapter in the author's voice with the promise on the cover baked in. |
| 3 | `podcast-guest-prep` | Prep the author for a podcast interview as guest. |
| 4 | `keynote-script` | Draft a 20-45 minute keynote script tuned to the venue. |
| 5 | `launch-runway` | Full launch runway from 90 days out to 30 days post-launch. |
| 6 | `testimonial-request` | Ask for and structure book testimonials. |
| 7 | `evergreen-pipeline` | Post-launch evergreen content pipeline. |
| 8 | `interrogate-decision` | Grill a proposed decision with the questions the author's editor or agent will ask. |
| 9 | `manuscript-audit` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Developmental audit: thesis clarity, target reader, cover promise delivery, structural coherence, voice consistency, duplication. |
| 10 | `book-endorsement-outreach` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Four-part outreach sequence to secure blurbs, forewords, and back-cover quotes. |
| 11 | `book-pitch-guest-appearance` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Pitch email + follow-up + pre-appearance prep for guesting on podcasts and media. |
| 12 | `newsletter-list-warming` | **NEW v1.1.0.** 30-day warming sequence before a launch, event, or offer. |

1. Download `author-bundle-v1.1.0.zip` from the newsletter link.
2. Unzip.
3. Claude Desktop, Settings, Skills, Add Skill, Upload folder.
4. Point at the `skills/` folder inside the unzipped bundle.
