Sixteen installable skills for the executive: the 5-Stage Operator Lens, dream escalation, board deck draft, investor update, hiring loop, decision quality check, hard conversation, quarterly strategy reset, principle check, Fractional CxO mode, executive-tier ghost-writer, weekly executive review, interrogate-decision, plus the three new v1.1.0 additions: friction audit, all-hands remarks, and layoff comms.

Built for VP through C-suite at $5M+ revenue.

## Anthropic Recommends

Anthropic has not shipped an executive-tier default set. AI Expert ships the first canonical set. The Executive Bundle extends Anthropic's baseline (Essential Bundle) with three specific executive-tier defaults:

- `human-approval-gate` (Essential Bundle prerequisite) gates every board-facing artifact and every layoff communication before delivery.
- `layoff-comms` locks the standard higher than most corporate playbooks. Zero euphemisms. Named responsibility.
- `all-hands-remarks` prevents the corporate-speak drift that AI drafts tend toward at scale.

## The sixteen skills

| # | Skill | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `five-stage-operator-lens` | Vision, Strategy, Planning, Execution, Tracking — operate at the right altitude. |
| 2 | `dream-escalation` | 10x, 100x, 1000x escalation on any idea. |
| 3 | `board-deck-draft` | Board deck with the right frame: what changed, what's next, what we're asking for. |
| 4 | `investor-update` | Monthly investor update in the operator's voice. |
| 5 | `hiring-loop` | Design and run a role loop end to end. |
| 6 | `decision-quality-check` | Score a pending decision against the operator's own quality bar. |
| 7 | `hard-conversation` | Prep a hard conversation with a report, peer, or partner. |
| 8 | `quarterly-strategy-reset` | Reset strategy against reality. |
| 9 | `principle-check` | Test a decision against the operator's stated principles. |
| 10 | `fractional-cxo-mode` | Switch Claude into Fractional CFO, COO, or CMO mode with the right lens. |
| 11 | `ghost-writer-me-exec` | Draft in the executive's voice at exec register. |
| 12 | `weekly-executive-review` | Friday exec review across the whole scope. |
| 13 | `interrogate-decision` | Grill a proposed decision with the questions the operator will get in the QBR. |
| 14 | `friction-audit` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Audit any workflow for meeting, approval, tool, handoff, and cognitive friction. Aaron Levie's insight. |
| 15 | `all-hands-remarks` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Draft executive remarks for an all-hands or town-hall in five parts. |
| 16 | `layoff-comms` | **NEW v1.1.0.** Full comms package for a layoff or restructure: affected-person script, all-hands remarks, leadership FAQ, external note, follow-up check-ins. |
