## What this is

Twelve installable skills for podcast hosts running authenticity-focused shows. Guest research, interview question designer, show notes writer, episode teaser clips, guest outreach, guest follow-up, episode planning cadence, show brand arc, sponsor pitch, podcast transcription workflow, interview recovery, and interrogate-decision.

Built for the Apex Podcast Co ICP: authenticity-focused musicians, real estate operators, and executives referred by PodcastNetwork.org. Not built for the SaaS-founder pitch-your-fund podcast pattern.

## Anthropic Recommends

Anthropic has not shipped a podcast-host default set. AI Expert ships the first canonical set. The Podcast Host Bundle extends Anthropic's baseline (Essential Bundle) with:

- Every guest outreach, sponsor pitch, and guest follow-up routes through `human-approval-gate` before send.
- The `podcast-transcription-workflow` skill covers the redaction pass explicitly — credentials, live customer names, and guest-requested cuts are handled before publish.
- The `interview-recovery` skill explicitly names the "guest reveals something they will regret" case and prescribes the post-recording flag protocol.

## The twelve skills

| # | Skill | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `guest-research` | Deep guest brief: public work, private wins, current focus, unspoken tensions, three questions no other host has asked. |
| 2 | `interview-question-designer` | Three-act flight: warm-up, substance with follow-up probes, close with the audience CTA. |
| 3 | `show-notes-writer` | Episode title, description, chapter markers, guest bio, key quotes, links, SEO metadata. |
| 4 | `episode-teaser-clips` | Clip target list, platform format spec, hook lines, posting cadence. |
| 5 | `guest-outreach` | Pitch + follow-up + calendar handoff for booking a guest. |
| 6 | `guest-followup` | Same-day thank-you, episode drop, promo-clip send, 90-day check-in. |
| 7 | `episode-planning-cadence` | 90-day rolling schedule with recording, publish, and promo days. |
| 8 | `show-brand-arc` | Show through-line, audience served, themes for the season, how the audience is meant to change. |
| 9 | `sponsor-pitch` | Show one-sheet, brand-match rationale, pitch email, follow-up. Real-audience positioning, not vanity reach. |
| 10 | `podcast-transcription-workflow` | Riverside / Descript / Whisper / human editor choice, accuracy pass, redaction pass, delivery. |
| 11 | `interview-recovery` | The four failure modes and the specific host moves for each. |
| 12 | `interrogate-decision` | Grill a proposed decision (guest booking, sponsor deal, show pivot) with the questions Brett or a partner would ask. |
